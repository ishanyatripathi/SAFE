CLINICAL PROBLEM

Sterility demands contact‑free control

In sterile environments (operating theatres), traditional interfaces like touchscreens and mice are major contamination risks.

Inaccessible for many

Motor impairments or bulky PPE make standard input impossible.

Voice & pedals fall short

Unreliable in noisy settings, lack precision, and introduce latency.

Breaking sterility = risk

Leads to contamination, workflow delays, and assistant dependency.

→ A reliable, intuitive, hands‑free control system is critically needed.
1/5 · problem
S.A.F.E. STERILE ADAPTIVE FREE ENGAGEMENT

Two‑pillar AI control

hands_module

  • left‑hand 2D scroll
  • right‑hand pinch‑to‑zoom
  • visual overlay & adaptive smoothing

head_module

  • nose‑lean for zoom
  • head‑turn for scroll
  • real‑time calibration · 6‑axis smoothing
